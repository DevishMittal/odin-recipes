Panner Handi

Description

Handi paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (cottage cheese) cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. The dish is usually prepared in a handi, a traditional Indian cooking vessel that adds an earthy flavor to the dish. The paneer is first marinated with spices and yogurt, then cooked in the handi along with onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and a blend of aromatic spices such as garam masala, cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Finally, a generous amount of cream is added to give the dish a smooth and velvety texture. Handi paneer is best served with hot naan bread or steamed rice, and garnished with fresh coriander leaves. It is a flavorful and satisfying vegetarian dish that is loved by many.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Yogurt
  • Cream
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee (clarified butter)

Steps

  1. Cut the paneer into small cubes and marinate them with yogurt, salt, and spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a handi or a deep pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger and garlic and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  6. Add garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add marinated paneer cubes and mix gently with the gravy.
  8. Cover the handi with a lid and let it cook for 5-7 minutes on low heat.
  9. Add cream and mix gently. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan bread or steamed rice.
